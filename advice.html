A Little Bit of Advice

I learned a lot throughout college. I start as a clueless, stressed out freshman at Columbia, and now I'm a slightly less clueless, slightly less stressed out PhD student at the University of Pennsylvania. I have pursued research for four academic years and two summers (including an undergraduate thesis), interned at Google, and TAed for high school, undergraduate, and graduate students in computer science. I remember how scary college can be. I hope my little stories help make college a little less scary for all of you.
